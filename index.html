Sarah Dykstra

Sarah Dykstra is a Research Associate at the Center for Global Development, where she supports the research of Charles Kenny and Justin Sandefur. Before joining CGD, she worked as a project associate for Innovations for Poverty Action in Sierra Leone, where she managed a random impact evaluation of two health service delivery programs. She was a Trustee Scholar at Boston University and graduated with a BA in global health and development; her thesis evaluates HIV/AIDS services in Malawi. Dykstra also spent a summer working in Syria on an education initiative for Iraqi refugees.
